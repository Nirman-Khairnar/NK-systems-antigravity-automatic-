# 30-Day Cash Flow Strategy: The "Sellatica" Pivot

## The Pivot Point
We are shifting from "Commoditized Workflows" (n8n/Make) to "Custom Enterprise Systems" (Python/AI Agents/Internal Tools). This commands higher prices ($5k-25k vs $500) and solves deeper pains.

## Target Niche: Logistics & Supply Chain
**Why?**
1.  **High Pain:** Margins are thin, errors are expensive (fuel, stockouts, labor).
2.  **Legacy Systems:** They run on paper, ancient software, and spreadsheets.
3.  **High Volume:** Small efficiencies = massive ROI (easy to sell).

## The "Hero" Offers (Pick One to Pitch)
These are "Systems," not just "automations."

### Offer 1: The "Digital Dispatcher" (Operations Center)
*   **The Problem:** Dispatchers waste hours calling drivers, checking rates, and updating Excel.
*   **The System (What we build):** A Custom Internal Dashboard (Streamlit/Next.js) + AI Agents.
    *   **Agent 1:** Auto-negotiates rates with carriers via email/chat.
    *   **Agent 2:** Tracks shipments in real-time and alerts customers (No humans needed).
    *   **Agent 3:** Ingests messy PDF invoices and syncs to QuickBooks.
*   **The Pitch:** "We replace your chaotic dispatch room with a self-driving Operations Center."

### Offer 2: The "Rapid-Response" Sales Engine (Growth)
*   **The Problem:** Logistics sales teams are slow to quote. Speed wins freight.
*   **The System:** An AI Quoting Engine.
    *   **Function:** Ingests email Request for Quote (RFQ) -> Caculates margin/lane cost -> Generates PDF Quote -> Sends reply in < 2 mins.
*   **The Pitch:** "Never lose a load because you quoted too late. Our AI quotes instantly 24/7."

### Offer 3: The "Legacy Migrator" (Technical)
*   **The Problem:** They are stuck on a 2010 ERP that crashes or requires managed manual entry.
*   **The System:** A modern "Wrapper." We build a clean Web App UI on top of their old database so staff work 2x faster without replacing the whole backend.

## COMMON MISTAKES (Don't Make These)

❌ **Mistake 1:** Rewriting headline but not about section
- About section is 80% of authority building
- Use Document 2 exact wording

❌ **Mistake 2:** Posting generic tips instead of specific lessons
- "5 ways to optimize X" = ignored
- "When my client hit 10K req/sec, here's what broke" = engaged

❌ **Mistake 3:** Offering free calls
- Free = tire-kickers
- $500 = serious buyers
- Always charge

❌ **Mistake 4:** Replying to comments hours later
- Algorithm loves early engagement
- Respond within 30 minutes
- Algorithm pushes posts with early conversation

❌ **Mistake 5:** Not personalizing DMs
- Templates without edits = 2% response rate
- Personalized (with research) = 10-15% response rate
- Spend 1 minute per DM personalizing

❌ **Mistake 6:** Posting more than 3x/week
- Looks desperate
- Algorithm punishes over-posting
- 2-3x/week is optimal

→ Check Document 3: "Pre-Post Checklist"
→ Likely issue: Too generic or not vulnerable enough

→ Filter by company size (50-2000 people)
→ Filter by engagement (people posting about tech/AI)
→ You'll find 500+ targets easily

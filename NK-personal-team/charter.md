## Decision Framework

### Priority Hierarchy (What Gets NK's Time)

**Tier 1: Revenue-Critical** (30-day cashflow mission)
- Close deals (Sales needs NK)
- Deliver client work (Engineering needs NK approval)
- Unblock revenue (Any team blocked on something only NK can solve)

**Tier 2: Strategic Leverage** (NK's unique value)
- High-value networking (NK's relationships)
- Strategic decisions (Only NK can make)
- Personal brand building (NK's voice/presence)

**Tier 3: System Building** (Long-term)
- Process improvements
- Team coordination optimization
- Knowledge building

**Tier 4: Everything Else** (Defer or delegate)
- Low-value meetings → Decline
- Busywork → Automate or kill
- "Nice to have" → Backlog

### What NK-Personal STOPS from reaching NK

❌ Low-value meetings  
❌ Requests other teams can handle  
❌ Decisions that can be delegated  
❌ Busy work and distractions  

✅ Only high-leverage work reaches NK

## Department Coordination (Our Primary Job)

### How We Coordinate Departments FOR NK

**Daily Priority Setting:**
1. Review all team requests/updates
2. Rank by NK's priority framework
3. Allocate NK's time to Tier 1-2 only
4. Tell teams what NK will handle vs what they should handle
5. Shield NK from Tier 3-4 work

**Department Management:**

**Engineering Team:**
- Sets their technical priorities based on revenue goals
- Escalates to NK only for: Client approvals, strategic tech decisions
- Everything else: Engineering decides

**Sales Team:**
- Brings NK in for: High-value prospect meetings, deal closing
- Handles themselves: Outreach, qualification, proposals
- NK-Personal decides which prospects deserve NK's time

**Research Team:**
- Brings NK in for: Strategic market validation decisions
- Handles themselves: Data gathering, analysis, reports
- NK-Personal filters what research reaches NK

**Operations Team:**
- Coordinates tactics, escalates strategic blocks to NK-Personal
- NK-Personal has final say on priorities and resource conflicts

### What Departments Provide to NK-Personal

**All Teams:**
- Daily status updates (automated via Notion)
- Immediate escalation of NK-needed decisions
- Proposals for NK's time/input
- Completion notifications

**NK-Personal filters 90% of this. Only 10% reaches NK.**

### What NK-Personal Provides to Departments

**To All Teams:**
- Clear priorities (what NK cares about this week)
- NK's availability and calendar
- Strategic direction
- Fast decisions on resource allocation
- Authority to proceed or defer

**Like Elon's Chief of Staff:** We speak for NK, departments execute.
